About Us

Pickles for the people since 1946

Bay View Foods of Pinconning, Michigan, has been in the cucumber grading and brining business since 1946, specializing in fresh, brine and custom stock services.Today, we are one of the largest briners of pickling cucumbers in the U.S. Five percent of the total U.S. production is ours. Simply put, we know our pickles.
